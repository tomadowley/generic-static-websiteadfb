Mission brief

Break the lockdown

Find the red access key, fight through the lab, and activate the exit gate. This is a clean-room, original implementation with procedural walls, sprites, weapons, pickups, HUD, minimap, enemy AI, and levels.

  • Raycast 3D renderer with floor and ceiling shading
  • Original maze, enemies, pickups, doors, and exit logic
  • Mouse-look, keyboard controls, damage, ammo, armor, and scoring
